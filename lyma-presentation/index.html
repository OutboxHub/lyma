Hello Kampala!

Lyma is a platform that helps farmers to determine the appropriate soil types for the crops they intend to plant. This will enable them to get better yields and in a way enhances their profits and provides a means to improve livelihoods for smallholder farmers.

Here is the problem?

Most farmers in Uganda have little knowledge about crops that are suitable for their land. Not knowing the suitable crop for the soil type in your land can gradually reduce the potential yield. Planting a particular crop in unsuitable soil type may lead to less or no yields thus wasting resources.

How we solved it!

Lyma app enlights farmers to know the right crop for their soil type. Having the right knowledge about what to plant in your land enables you to plant just that. For example knowing that maize does not give you the best results in swampy areas enables you to opt for another type of crop. The app can also help farmers to know if they should add fertilizers or nutrient supplements to their land.
